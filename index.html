THE TEAM

Davi Lemos

Project Lead

Davi leads the Voluntoken team in all it's endeavors, strategizing the path ahead and facilitating the work of all members as we strive to redefine the volunteering and local-economies ecosystem. In the past Davi has been an AIESEC Local Committee President for two terms, co-lead the Brazil Club at Yale, head-organized a TEDx event, and started his own samba group. He is also the co-founder of the Blockchain Undergraduate Collective at Yale and is a current Board Member at the Yale Blockchain Initiative.

Fernando Alonso Bilfinger

Co-Founder and Outreach Leader

I am first-year student double majoring in Economics and Statistics & Data Science at Yale University and currently work as the Outreach and Partnerships Leader at VolunToken. My interest in economics along with community service experiences as the founder for educational programs and websites in Brazilian NGOs and as the current Dwight Hall International Network Coordinator for social service and justice serve VolunToken with customer insights and important connections with local businesses and NGOs.
